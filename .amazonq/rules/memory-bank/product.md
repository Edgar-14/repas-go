# BeFast GO - Driver Mobile App

## Project Overview
BeFast GO is a React Native mobile application designed for delivery drivers in the BeFast ecosystem. The app provides comprehensive tools for managing delivery operations, tracking earnings, and ensuring compliance with Mexican labor regulations (IMSS).

## Core Value Proposition
- **Real-time Order Management**: Accept, track, and complete delivery orders efficiently
- **Integrated Navigation**: GPS-powered routing to pickup and delivery locations
- **Digital Wallet**: Track earnings, tips, and payment history with detailed breakdowns
- **IMSS Compliance**: Built-in validation and compliance with Mexican labor requirements
- **Communication Hub**: Direct messaging with customers and support team

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Order Management
- Real-time order notifications and acceptance
- Order details with pickup/delivery locations
- Status tracking throughout delivery lifecycle
- Order history and completion ratings
- Emergency incident reporting

### Navigation & Location
- Integrated GPS navigation to destinations
- Real-time location tracking for customers
- Route optimization and directions
- Location-based order assignment

### Financial Management
- Digital wallet with earnings tracking
- Detailed payment breakdowns (base pay, tips, bonuses)
- Payment history and transaction records
- Earnings analytics and metrics

### Driver Experience
- Comprehensive onboarding process
- Document management and verification
- Performance metrics and ratings
- Profile and settings management
- Push notifications for orders and updates

### Communication
- In-app chat with customers
- AI-powered chatbot assistance
- Support ticket system
- Emergency contact features

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Delivery drivers working with BeFast platform
- **Secondary**: Fleet managers and operations teams
- **Geographic Focus**: Mexico (with IMSS compliance requirements)

## Use Cases
1. **Daily Operations**: Drivers use the app to receive, accept, and complete delivery orders
2. **Earnings Tracking**: Monitor daily/weekly earnings and payment breakdowns
3. **Navigation**: Get turn-by-turn directions to pickup and delivery locations
4. **Customer Communication**: Chat with customers about delivery details
5. **Compliance**: Maintain required documentation and labor law compliance
6. **Performance Monitoring**: Track ratings, completion rates, and metrics
